How long does it take to build a website?
Most custom websites are completed in 4–8 weeks depending on scope and complexity. We'll give you a clear timeline upfront.
Will my website be mobile responsive?
Absolutely. Every site we build is fully responsive and optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop from the ground up.
How long does SEO take to show results?
SEO is a long-term strategy. Most clients begin seeing meaningful improvements in rankings and traffic within 3–6 months.
Do you offer website maintenance after launch?
Yes. We offer ongoing maintenance plans that cover updates, security, performance monitoring, and content changes.
What's included in an e-commerce solution?
We build fully custom stores with product pages, checkout flows, payment integrations, and inventory management tailored to your brand.
Do I need to provide all the content and copy?
Not necessarily. We can work with your existing content or help craft compelling copy that aligns with your brand voice.
What platforms do you build on?
We build on WordPress, Webflow, Shopify, and fully custom-coded solutions — whatever best fits your goals and budget.
Can I update the website myself after it's built?
Yes. We include training so you're confident managing your site. We build with user-friendly CMS tools that don't require technical skills.
What makes Social Swarm different from other agencies?
We're founder-focused and move at your speed. No bloated teams, no endless meetings — just high-quality work delivered fast with direct communication.
Do you offer ongoing support after the project is complete?
Yes. We offer flexible retainer packages for ongoing support, whether that's monthly updates, SEO, or new feature development.
